General
-------
1) Mouse starts in starting position (S5)
2) Mouse follows its policy (probabilistic actions)
  - Policy is random in the beginning
  - Policy over time will depend on the implemented algorithm
3) Mouse reaches new state depending on the policy and on chance
4) Mouse receives reward depending on the new state
5) repeat 2)-4) until mouse reaches cheese
6) If mouse reaches cheese, set mouse back to starting position (S5)
